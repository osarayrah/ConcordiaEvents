Concordia Events

A mobile-first event discovery interface for Concordia students, focused on clear information architecture, approachable event browsing, and lightweight social coordination.

Design Objective

The visual system is intended to feel reliable, modern, and easy to navigate for students comparing academic, social, career, and wellness events from a single interface.

Design System

  • Concordia-inspired burgundy and gold palette
  • Rounded card surfaces with restrained depth
  • High-contrast hierarchy for quick scanning
  • Mobile-first browsing patterns and persistent navigation
Discovery

Search and Filtering

Search, category chips, and filters are placed early in the flow to reduce effort during event selection.

Structure

Clear Content Hierarchy

Recommended, trending, and upcoming sections help separate intent while keeping the feed straightforward.
