Concordia Events Platform

Discover Concordia campus life in one place.

Concordia Events is a concept website and mobile experience that helps students find academic, social, career, and wellness events without searching across multiple disconnected channels.

Check Out Our App View Features
Problem

Fragmented discovery

Students often rely on Instagram, Discord, email, and posters to track what is happening on campus.

Goal

Simple coordination

The platform combines event discovery with lightweight social planning so students can feel more confident attending.

Outcome

One clear interface

A mobile-first system built around browsing, filtering, saving, and organizer publishing in one place.

Key Features

Designed for student discovery and coordination

The product focuses on fast browsing, event clarity, and low-friction coordination for Concordia students.

Why it matters

Built for everyday student behavior

Instead of expecting students to change their habits, the product is designed around how they already look for opportunities: quickly, socially, and on mobile.

Experience focus

Speed, clarity, and confidence

Each screen is meant to reduce friction between seeing an event, understanding it, and deciding whether to attend.

Feature 01

Personalized discovery

Interest-based recommendations surface events relevant to each student’s academic and social goals.

Feature 02

Fast filtering

Search, category chips, and targeted filters help students narrow options without leaving the feed.

Feature 03

Social confidence

Friends attending, invite actions, and light planning details reduce uncertainty before showing up.

Feature 04

Organizer efficiency

Clubs and organizers can publish events quickly through a concise and mobile-friendly creation flow.

Design System

Professional, mobile-first visual language

The interface uses a Concordia-inspired palette, strong typographic hierarchy, and a consistent card system across the site and app.

Typography

Clear hierarchy at a glance

The website and app use strong headlines, restrained labels, and readable body copy so students can scan quickly without feeling overwhelmed.

Layout

Consistent cards and spacing

Shared card structure, repeatable spacing, and mobile-first proportions help the interface stay coherent across sections.

System

Visual Direction

Concordia-inspired burgundy and gold create a distinct academic identity while preserving a polished digital feel.

System

Interface Style

Rounded cards, layered surfaces, and strong typographic hierarchy help the interface feel modern and approachable.

System

Navigation

Persistent bottom navigation and clear sectioning support fast movement across the product without added complexity.

System

Prototype Focus

The concept prioritizes UI quality and user flow rather than backend implementation details.
